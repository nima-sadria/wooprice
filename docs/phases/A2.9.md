# A2.9 — AI Foundation

## Phase

A2.9

## Name

AI Foundation

## Status

NOT STARTED

## Mission

Provide advisory intelligence that assists pricing decisions while remaining entirely outside the trusted execution path. The AI Foundation generates signals, warnings, and recommendations for human review only. It has no authority to apply prices, override safety policies, bypass seller confirmation, or trigger any execution.

---

## Scope

- Error detection service (identify anomalies in proposed prices, rule outputs, or source data)
- Market freshness alerting (signal when source data may be stale relative to expected market conditions)
- Risk warning service (flag high-risk proposals for human attention before confirmation)
- Recommendation support layer (provide structured AI recommendations alongside Price Proposals for human review)
- Advisory output model (structured advisory record: signal type, confidence, affected items, recommended action)
- Advisory repository (persistence of all AI advisory outputs)
- Advisory audit log (every AI output recorded with model version, inputs, outputs, and timestamp)

## Out of Scope

- Autonomous price application (no auto-apply)
- Safety policy override (no AI-driven safety policy bypass)
- Seller confirmation bypass (no AI-triggered confirmation)
- WooCommerce write operations (no destination writes by AI components)
- Autonomous execution (no AI-initiated Change Set execution)
- Rule generation without Owner approval (no AI-created pricing rules without explicit Owner authorization)
- Autonomous business-policy modification (no AI changes to business rules)
- Autonomous safety-policy modification (no AI changes to safety thresholds or modes)
- Any modification to existing Workspace, Apply, Dry Run, or Safety Engine logic

---

## Architecture Rules

- All AI outputs are advisory only; no AI output may be used as direct input to any execution path without explicit human review and confirmation.
- The AI Foundation must be architecturally isolated from the trusted execution path (A2.5 Change Set, A2.6 Dry Run, A2.7 Execution, A2.8 Scheduling); no AI component may call or modify any component in the trusted execution path.
- Every AI recommendation must be persisted with: model version, input snapshot reference, output, confidence score, and timestamp.
- AI components must not hold or cache credentials for WooCommerce or any destination system.
- Rule generation by AI is forbidden unless the Owner explicitly approves the specific rule and it is persisted via the Rule Engine (A2.3) through the standard workflow.
- Business-policy and safety-policy modification by AI is permanently forbidden without explicit Owner approval for each specific change.
- No AI output may suppress or override a Safety Policy `BLOCK` or `REQUIRE OVERRIDE` result.

## Production Safety Rules

**Global A2 Rules — apply to every A2 phase:**

- A2 must remain additive until reconciliation and parity verification pass.
- Every phase must remain independently auditable.
- Each phase must preserve backward compatibility with the existing Workspace until production cutover is explicitly approved.
- No phase may introduce functionality belonging to future phases.
- No production cutover without explicit Owner approval.
- No production deployment during A2 development unless explicitly approved by the Owner.
- No Apply integration outside its approved phase.
- No Dry Run integration outside its approved phase.
- No Safety Engine integration outside its approved phase.
- No Change Set logic outside its approved phase.
- No WooCommerce write logic outside its approved phase.
- Protected systems always require explicit Owner approval before modification.

**Protected Systems — require explicit Owner approval before modification:**

- Workspace UI
- Preview Engine
- Existing Cache Synchronization
- Apply Workflow
- Dry Run Workflow
- Emergency Apply
- JWT/Auth
- Pricing Validation
- WooCommerce Write Path
- Database Migrations
- Maintenance Mode
- Production Deployment
- Rollback / Undo

---

## Owner Decisions

- **No auto apply.** AI components may never trigger execution.
- **No safety override.** AI components may never bypass or suppress a Safety Policy result.
- **No seller confirmation bypass.** AI components may never act as a seller confirmation.
- **No WooCommerce write.** AI components hold no destination credentials and make no destination writes.
- **No autonomous execution.** AI components may not initiate, schedule, or trigger any Change Set execution.
- **No rule generation without Owner approval.** AI may not create or publish pricing rules without explicit Owner authorization for each specific rule.
- **No autonomous business-policy modification.** AI may not modify business rules.
- **No autonomous safety-policy modification.** AI may not modify safety thresholds or policy modes.
